Dyes

In recent years, sensitizing dyes for usage in dye sensitized solar cells have attracted considerable attention. Although much work is required to reach optimal device efficiencies, significant effort has gone into improving the photophysical and photochemical properties of dyes. In addition to the dye, changes to the cell fabrication process and modifications to other components such as the photoanode, electrolyte and counter electrodes impact both the efficiency and stability of DSSCs.
